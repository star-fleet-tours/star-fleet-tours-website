SpaceX Starship Flight 6

Join Star Fleet for the next launch and booster catch of the SpaceX Starship, the most powerful rocket in history! It'll be launching no earlier than Monday November 18, 2024 at 4:00 PM CST (22:00 UTC) on the Flight 6 mission, which is expected to feature another spectacular mechazilla catch attempt of the Super Heavy booster. We'll be offering boat trips to view the launch, as well as a special first-come, first-serve pre-launch cruise—reach out to us on our Whats App community or at 321-209-2224 if interested in the latter.

Our viewing location at the mouth of the South Bay, approximately 4 miles / 7 km from the launch site with a direct line of sight across the water, provides some of the closest and clearest views of the pad, rocket, liftoff and booster landing available to the public. Note that unlike the location just off the South Padre jetty where most other vessels anchor, this offers a closer and slightly clearer sightline, has calmer, more protected and less busy waters, and doesn't have many other large boats to block our view, nor do we block that of those watching from shore.

LATEST LAUNCH UPDATE: As of Wednesday, November 6, SpaceX has officially confirmed Starship's next launch and landing is on for Monday November 18, at a late afternoon launch time of 4:00 PM CST (22:00 UTC). Their existing FAA license is valid for this attempt and FAA and marine hazard zones have been published, with booster and ship testing complete and undergoing preparations for flight. We're in the final stages of locking down a boat as well as a more generous scrub policy, so we've set tickets to go live this Friday, November 8, at 12 noon CST (18:00 UTC). If you have any questions, you're welcome to reach out on Whats App (below) or at the Star Fleet number 321-209-2224.

We have a Whats App community that we highly recommend you join, where folks are coordinating carpooling, accommodations, travel tips and more, as well as for updates from and questions to the Star Fleet organizers! It has separate channels for major Star Fleet announcements, Flight 6 updates, launch discussion, Q&A and general chat, so you can subscribe to notifications for only what you're interested in. We highly recommend all passengers join it to get the fastest updates and responses directly from us! Be sure to check our Twitter, Instagram and Facebook for the latest updates on all our activities, as well as announcements of smaller/informal trips, and feel free to reach out over DM if you're interested in a private charter for a specific mission. Thanks!

Rocket Launch Viewing

Price: $99 per passenger
Launch Date: No Earlier Than 4:00 PM CST (22:00 UTC) November 18 2024
Key Times: Checkin 2:00 PM | Boarding 2:30 PM | Departure 3:00 PM
Location: TBA, Port Isabel/South Padre Island, TX

  • Great location to view all the key events of the launch
  • Around 4 miles / 7 km from launch pad
  • Clear view of pad, rocket, liftoff and ascent
  • Stable vessel, calm seas and open deck for excellent viewing
  • Free parking, a guaranteed spot and no need to show up many hours early

    DELAY/SCRUB POLICY: Tickets are generally non-refundable.
    Per agreement with our captains, if there is a delay announced prior to boarding the boat, your ticket will be valid for the new launch date and time. If you can’t make the backup date, you are welcome to sell your ticket to others on standby, and we can help facilitate that as well. If a launch day delay/scrub is officially announced after passengers have boarded the boat or if a passenger does not show up by the published boarding time, the boat will go out as scheduled, tickets will be considered used and will no longer be refundable. Conversely, case there is an issue with the boat or weather keeping us from departing for the launch, and it occurs as scheduled, a full refund will be issued.
