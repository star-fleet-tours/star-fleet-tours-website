SpaceX Crew Dragon DM-2

As of March 6, the Falcon 9/Crew Dragon DM-2 launch to the International Space Station is currently scheduled for No Earlier Than Thursday, May 7 from historic launch pad LC-39A at NASA's Kennedy Space Center. This milestone launch will feature the first human spaceflight from the US in nearly a decade since the Space Shuttle, and the first crewed orbital mission on a commercial spacecraft.

Star✦Fleet will offer a clear view of the pad, liftoff and ascent into orbit from a favorable angle and as close or closer than most or all other locations that do not require >$200+ KSCVC tickets, along with dedicated free parking, full facilities and reserved spots with the need to arrive as little as an hour in advance of launch. We'll also be offering exclusive closeup VIP pre-launch boat tours of the rocket and spacecraft on the pad (timing permitting), as well as the special NASA launch day cookies baked by Julia Bergeron herself from the same recipe and tradition handed down to her as for the astronauts since Apollo.

STATUS UPDATE: Check back closer to the mission, once the date, safety zones and boat availability is finalized, for more details about Star Fleet's offerings on this launch! We aim the have ticket available for sale at the two week mark from a confirmed launch date, and will announce the exact date and time of availability well in advance so everyone can be prepared to order on what is likely to be a very popular mission.

Make sure to stay tuned to our Slack, email list and social media for the latest important updates and to get in touch with us directly (see below for links). Thanks!
