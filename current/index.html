SpaceX Starship Flight 12 & NASA/SpaceX CRS-12 Launch

Join Star Fleet for the first-ever flight of the new Starship and Super Heavy Version 3, and the first launch from the brand new Pad 2! The current earliest possible launch date for Starship Flight 12 is No Earlier Than Tuesday May 19 at 5:30 PM CDT (22:30 UTC) for the mission, although it is expected to slip, and we'll be out there to view it!

Our planned Starship launch viewing location at the mouth of the South Bay, approximately 4 miles / 7 km from the launch site with a direct line of sight across the water, provides some of the closest and clearest views of the pad, rocket, liftoff and booster landing available to the public. Unlike the location where most other vessels anchor, this offers a closer and clearer sightline, and has calmer, less busy waters without the need to constantly jockey for position.

Meanwhile, SpaceX has a confirmed launch date of Tuesday May 12 at 7:16 PM EDT (23:16 UTC) for the CRS-34 Cargo Dragon mission for NASA to the International Space Station, which will feature a spectacular booster landing back at the launch site. We plan to offer both launch and pre-launch sunset trips for this mission which can get as close as 4 mi / 7 km to launch and landing (and 500-1000 yd/m from the rocket and spacecraft on the pad), depending on demand. Reach out if you are interested!

LATEST LAUNCH UPDATE: As of Tuesday May 12, further updated hazard zones indicate a Starship Flight 12 launch window now opening as early as May 19, from 5:30-7:30 PM CDT (22:30-00:30 UTC) each day. The full stack dress rehearsal is now apparently complete, although additional work and testing is still believed to be needed before flight. With that news, we are in the final stages of confirm boat avaibility and pricing, and plan to open up tickets this evening at 6 PM CDT, when the link will go live below. As always, keep an eye here, on our social media or our WhatsApp community for the latest updates!

Meanwhile, the NASA/SpaceX CRS-34 launch and landing of the Cargo Dragon capsule to the space station continues to be on track for today, May 12 at 7:16 PM EDT (23:16 UTC), although tickets are currently SOLD OUT. It'll feature a closeup view of the launch and landing on this mission!

We have a Whats App community that we highly recommend you join, with channels for announcements, insider launch updates, discussion, questions and more! SpaceX Starship Flight 12 Launch Viewing

Price: ~$99 per passenger
Launch Date: No Earlier Than Tuesday May 19 at 5:30 PM CDT
Key Times: Checkin 3:30 PM CDT | Boarding 4:00 PM CDT | Departure 4:30 PM CDT
Boarding Location: TBD, either Port Isabel or near Isla Blanca Park

  • Great location to view all the key events of the launch
  • Around ≈4 miles / ≈6.5 km from launch pad
  • Clear view of pad, rocket, liftoff and ascent
  • Stable vessel, calm seas and open deck for excellent viewing
  • Free parking, a guaranteed viewing spot and no need to show up many hours early
  • If scrubbed prior to checkin time, ticket still valid for the next attempt

    DELAY/SCRUB POLICY: Tickets are generally non-refundable.
    Per agreement with our captains, if there is a delay announced prior to the checkin time on the day of launch, your ticket will be valid for the new launch date and time. Should you not be able to make the backup date, you can either transfer your tickets to someone who can, or reach out to use by email at FleetCommand@Star-Fleet.Tours or phone at 941-879-4240 to request a refund (at least 24 hours in advance of launch). Should a launch day delay/scrub be officially announced after that or if a passenger does not show up by the published boarding time, the boat will go out as scheduled to see the rocket on the pad, and tickets will be considered used and will no longer be refundable, but you will be offered a minimum 10% (often higher) discount on future attempts of the same launch. Conversely, in case there is an issue with the boat or weather keeping us from departing for the launch, and it occurs as scheduled, we'll issue you a full refund.
