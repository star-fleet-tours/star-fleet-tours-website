NASA Space Launch System Pad Rollout

Star Fleet is currently planning to go out again for a sunset boat trips to see the NASA Space Launch System rocket on LC-39B, just before its Wet Dress Rehearsal, the final test before launch! Join us to see the world's most powerful completed rocket as it stands tall on its launch pad for the very first time.

Make sure to stay tuned to our Twitter feed, as well as our Slack, email list and social media for the latest important updates and to get in touch with us directly (see below for more links). Thanks!

SLS Launch Pad Sunset Trip

Price: $112 per person
Date: Friday April 1, 2022
Trip Times: 6:15 PM - 9:15 PM EDT
Key Times: Checkin T-30 min | Boarding T-15 min | Departure T-0 min
Location: Sunrise Marina, 505 Glen CHeek Dr, Port Canaveral FL 32920

  • See NASA's new SLS rocket, the most powerful in the world, standing on its launch pad for the first time, from an up-close vantage point and a unique angle
  • Chance to see one or potentially two SpaceX Falcon 9s on the pad, one with the Transporter-4 mission, and one with a Dragon capsule for Axiom-1, the first fully-commercial mission to the International Space Station (subject to launch schedules)
  • See historic Apollo and Shuttle sites, including LC-34, LC-36 and LC-39A/B
  • Enjoy a unique ocean-side view of every active CCAFS and KSC launch pad
  • View many sites of interest in Port Canaveral harbor and along the Cape
  • Comes with a genuine home-made NASA "launch day cookie" (see below)
  • Additional trips may be made available based on passenger demand


Home-Baked NASA Launch Day Cookies

Price: One free with pre-launch tour ticket; otherwise $2 donation to the SCLA
Order: Online, Onsite

  • Be part of a time-honored tradition stretching back to the Apollo astronauts
  • Made from the original NASA recipe used for launch-day cookies
  • Baked by the same person (Julia Bergeron), in the same batch as the cookies for the actual launch team for the mission
  • Buy and pickup fresh on site at launch checkin, and included with all pre-launch tours
  • All proceeds go directly to the Space Coast Launch Ambassadors
