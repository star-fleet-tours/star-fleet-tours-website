SpaceX Starlink 10-54

Join Star Fleet for Starlink 10-54, the next Falcon 9 launch building out SpaceX's groundbreaking internet constellation! The currently targeted launch date is No Earlier Than Friday June 12 at 8:27 AM EDT (12:27 UTC) for the mission, and we'll be out there to view it! We expect to be around ~3-4 mi / ~6-7 km away, with a clear view of the pad and liftoff.

LATEST LAUNCH UPDATE: As of Tuesday June 9, SpaceX is confirmed to be proceeding toward launch, with the vehicle being prepared for flight. Launch and viewing conditions look excellent, with mostly to completely clear skies, no rain or storms predicted, only a light breeze and relatively calm seas. We are currently working to confirm the ideal boats to run the trip based on demand, so you can call us to reserve your place for now. As always, keep an eye here, on our social media or our WhatsApp community for the latest updates.

SpaceX Starlink 10-54 Launch Viewing

Price: $125 per passenger
Launch Date: No Earlier Than Friday June 12 at 8:27 AM EDT
Key Times: Checkin 6:45 AM CDT | Boarding 7:00 AM CDT | Departure 7:15 AM CDT
Boarding Location: Sunrise Marina, 505 Glen Cheek Dr, Cape Canaveral, FL 32920

  • Great location to view all the key events of the launch
  • Around ≈3-4 miles / ≈6-7 km from launch pad
  • Get an exclusive angle from along the rocket's ocean-bound trajectory
  • Clear view of pad, rocket, liftoff and ascent
  • Stable vessel and calm seas expected
  • Free parking, a guaranteed viewing spot and no need to show up many hours early
  • If scrubbed prior to checkin time, ticket still valid for the next attempt

    DELAY/SCRUB POLICY: Tickets are generally non-refundable.
    Per agreement with our captains, if there is a delay announced prior to the checkin time on the day of launch, your ticket will be valid for the new launch date and time. Should you not be able to make the backup date, you can either transfer your tickets to someone who can, or reach out to use by email at FleetCommand@Star-Fleet.Tours or phone at 941-879-4240 to request a refund (at least 24 hours in advance of launch). Should a launch day delay/scrub be officially announced after that or if a passenger does not show up by the published boarding time, the boat will go out as scheduled to see the rocket on the pad, and tickets will be considered used and will no longer be refundable, but you will be offered a minimum 10% (often higher) discount on future attempts of the same launch. Conversely, in case there is an issue with the boat or weather keeping us from departing for the launch, and it occurs as scheduled, we'll issue you a full refund.
