SpaceX Crew Dragon DM-2

As of May 6, the Falcon 9/Crew Dragon DM-2 launch to the International Space Station is currently scheduled for No Earlier Than Thursday, May 27 at 20:27 UTC (4:27 PM EDT) from historic launch pad LC-39A at NASA's Kennedy Space Center. This milestone launch will feature the first human spaceflight from the US in nearly a decade since the Space Shuttle, and the first crewed orbital mission on a commercial spacecraft.

STATUS UPDATE: We're very excited about this mission, as are all of you, and we've been waiting and monitoring the COVID-19 situation and exploring what might be possible to do given these unprecedented times. Based on the guidance we've received, we've made the difficult decision to cancel the planned Star Fleet trip to view this launch, and echo the advice of NASA, public health experts and the spaceflight community to please enjoy this launch at home. Safety is our number one concern, and particularly given the relatively high risk profile of getting a large number of people from all around the country in a relatively modest sized space for an hour or more, no launch is worth risking people's health over.

If you're a local and are determined to view this launch, you could consider getting in touch with our Port Canaveral charter captains for a private trip, but we're not encouraging anyone to travel or expose themselves, their families or the community to the virus for this. While this is a historic moment, given the many exciting upcoming developments for NASA, SpaceX and other space companies and organizations, there will surely be many more to watch in person for many years to come, so we urge all of you to take care of your health now so we can all witness them together in the future. Thanks for being part of our Star Fleet family in these difficult times, and please reach out if you have further questions.

We hope you are able to join us on another trip soon, and until then, we hope you stay home, stay safe, and enjoy plenty of space!

Make sure to stay tuned to our Slack, email list and social media for the latest important updates and to get in touch with us directly (see below for links). Thanks!
